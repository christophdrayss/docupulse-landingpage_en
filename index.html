F.A.Q

Can I test Docupulse for free?

You can test Docupulse for free. Contact us and we’ll set up the software for a free 2-month period.

What security measures are in place to protect my data?

You can choose whether your documents are stored on your own Sharepoint/Azure account or on Docupulse's servers. Docupulse uses Microsoft Azure with high security standards, including encrypted data transfer and revision-safe archiving.

What custom solutions does the Ultimate Plan offer?

The Ultimate Plan includes all features of the Business Plan and offers custom solutions for data transformation to fit your specific workflows.

How do I install Docupulse?

Docupulse is easily installed via the Microsoft Add-In Store or as a customized solution for your servers.
