F.A.Q

Can I test Docupulse Exert Plan for free?

You can test Docupulse for free. Contact us, and we will set up the software for a 6-month trial period.

What security measures are taken to protect my data?

You can choose whether to store and process documents on your own SharePoint/Azure account or on Docupulse's servers. Docupulse uses Microsoft Azure as the server host with high security standards, including encrypted data transmission, secure storage, and audit-proof archiving.

What individual solutions does the Ultimate Plan offer?

The Ultimate Plan includes all the features of the Business Plan, plus tailored solutions for data transformation processes that are specifically aligned with your defined workflows. Our expert team works closely with you to ensure your specific needs are efficiently met.

How do I install Docupulse?

You can easily install Docupulse via the Microsoft Add-In Store or set it up as a custom server for your private servers.
