Cloud Setup

To connect Docupulse to your cloud, add Docupulse as a cloud member using the email address below in OneDrive or Google Drive. This ensures that the add-in only has access to specific folders, and you can revoke access at any time independently of Docupulse. Alternatively, you can also upload documents directly to Docupulse.
